Meet Kun Troll. Quietly.

One parametric character, three renderings, five locked expressions. Built once, reused everywhere — so he looks identical on every screen.

Direction: Kun Troll stays discreet. Corner-badge by default; the hero treatment is reserved for genuine moments — onboarding, a scene firing, Troll Scout finding something. Presence, not decoration. Pick a rendering below and the whole sheet updates.
